Finding Nemo

A clownfish is overly cautious with his son, Nemo, who has a foreshortened fin. When Nemo swims too close to the surface, he is caught by a diver. Marlin must set out to find him. A blue reef fish named Dory joins Marlin and complicates the encounters with sharks, jellyfish, and a host of ocean dangers. Meanwhile, Nemo plots his escape from a dentist's fish tank.
